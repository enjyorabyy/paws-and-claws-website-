About Us!

Paws & claws is an adoption website that connects animals in need with the right people. Based online, we work as an independent third party between those looking to rehome an animal and those wishing to adopt, foster or sponsor an animal.


Our mission extends beyond just finding homes for animals. We believe in the power of education and advocacy to promote responsible pet ownership and to address issues such as animal welfare, overpopulation, and neglect. Through our online resources, educational materials, and community outreach efforts, we strive to empower individuals to make informed decisions and to create a world where every animal is valued and cared for








You Are All What They Need!

By opening your heart and home, you not only provide shelter and love to an animal in need but also alleviate strain on overcrowded shelters. Your decision to adopt or foster a stray animal not only saves a life but also creates space for another animal to receive care.
